He would come to her in the afternoon or evening, sit and roll&nbsp;his cigarette, talk a little, and go away as he had done the night before.&nbsp;But how delicious it would be to have him there with her! She would have no&nbsp;regrets, nor seek to penetrate his reserve if he still chose to wear it.
