It was late when he left her. It was getting to be more than a passing whim&nbspwith Arobin to see her and be with her. He had detected the latent sensuality,&nbspwhich unfolded under his delicate sense of her nature’s requirements like&nbspa torpid, torrid, sensitive blossom.
There was no despondency when she fell asleep that night; nor was there hope&nbspwhen she awoke in the morning.
