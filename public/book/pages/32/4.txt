After a little while, a few days, in fact, Edna went up and spent a week with&nbsp;her children in Iberville. They were delicious February days, with all the&nbsp;summer’s promise hovering in the air.
How glad she was to see the children! She wept for very pleasure when she&nbsp;felt their little arms clasping her; their hard, ruddy cheeks pressed against&nbsp;her own glowing cheeks. She looked into their faces with hungry eyes that&nbsp;could not be satisfied with looking. And what stories they had to tell their&nbsp;mother! About the pigs, the cows, the mules!
