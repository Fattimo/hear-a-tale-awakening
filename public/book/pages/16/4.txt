There was no special message to Edna except a postscript&nbsp;saying that if Mrs. Pontellier desired to finish the book which he had been&nbsp;reading to her, his mother would find it in his room, among other books there&nbsp;on the table. Edna experienced a pang of jealousy because he had written to&nbsp;his mother rather than to her.
