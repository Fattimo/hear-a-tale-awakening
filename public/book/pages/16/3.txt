     “Oh, Robert stopped having his pictures taken when he had to pay for
them himself! He found wiser use for his money, he says,” explained Madame
Lebrun. She had a letter from him, written before he left New Orleans. Edna
wished to see the letter, and Madame Lebrun told her to look for it either
on the table or the dresser, or perhaps it was on the mantelpiece.
