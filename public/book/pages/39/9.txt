Exhaustion was pressing upon and overpowering her.
“Good-by—because I love you.” He did not know; he did not understand. He would&nbsp;never understand. Perhaps Doctor Mandelet would have understood if she had&nbsp;seen him—but it was too late; the shore was far behind her, and her strength&nbsp;was gone.
She looked into the distance, and the old terror flamed up for an instant,&nbsp;then sank again. Edna heard her father’s voice and her sister Margaret’s.&nbsp;She heard the barking of an old dog that was chained to the sycamore tree.&nbsp;The spurs of the cavalry officer clanged as he walked across the porch.
