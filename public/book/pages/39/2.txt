There were a dozen men crazy about her at the Chênière; and&nbsp;since it was the fashion to be in love with married people, why, she could&nbsp;run away any time she liked to New Orleans with Célina’s husband.
Célina’s husband was a fool, a coward, and a pig, and to prove it to her,&nbsp;Victor intended to hammer his head into a jelly the next time he encountered&nbsp;him. This assurance was very consoling to Mariequita. She dried her eyes,&nbsp;and grew cheerful at the prospect.
