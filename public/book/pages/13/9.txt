“Where are Madame Antoine and her son?” asked Edna.
“Gone to Vespers, and to visit some friends, I believe. I am to take you back&nbsp;in Tonie’s boat whenever you are ready to go.”
He stirred the smoldering ashes till the broiled fowl began to sizzle afresh.&nbsp;He served her with no mean repast, dripping the coffee anew and sharing it&nbsp;with her. Madame Antoine had cooked little else than the mullets, but while&nbsp;Edna slept Robert had foraged the island. He was childishly gratified to discover&nbsp;her appetite, and to see the relish with which she ate the food which he had&nbsp;procured for her.
“Shall we go right away?” she asked, after draining her glass and brushing&nbsp;together the crumbs of the crusty loaf.
