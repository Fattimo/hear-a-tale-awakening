Edna, left alone in the little side room, loosened her clothes, removing the&nbsp;greater part of them. She bathed her face, her neck and arms in the basin&nbsp;that stood between the windows. She took off her shoes and stockings and stretched&nbsp;herself in the very center of the high, white bed. How luxurious it felt to&nbsp;rest thus in a strange, quaint bed, with its sweet country odor of laurel&nbsp;lingering about the sheets and mattress! She stretched her strong limbs that&nbsp;ached a little.
