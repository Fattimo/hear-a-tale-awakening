Mrs. Pontellier liked to sit and gaze at her fair companion as she might look&nbspupon a faultless Madonna.
“Could any one fathom the cruelty beneath that fair exterior?” murmured Robert.&nbsp“She knew that I adored her once, and she let me adore her. It was ‘Robert,&nbspcome; go; stand up; sit down; do this; do that; see if the baby sleeps; my&nbspthimble, please, that I left God knows where. Come and read Daudet to me while&nbspI sew.’”
“Par exemple! I never had to ask. You were always there under my feet, like&nbspa troublesome cat.”
