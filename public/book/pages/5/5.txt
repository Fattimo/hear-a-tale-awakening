During his oblivious attention he once quietly rested his head against Mrs.&nbsp;Pontellier’s arm. As gently she repulsed him. Once again he repeated the offense.&nbsp;She could not but believe it to be thoughtlessness on his part; yet that was&nbsp;no reason she should submit to it. She did not remonstrate, except again to&nbsp;repulse him quietly but firmly. He offered no apology. The picture completed&nbsp;bore no resemblance to Madame Ratignolle. She was greatly disappointed to&nbsp;find that it did not look like her. But it was a fair enough piece of work,&nbsp;and in many respects satisfying.
