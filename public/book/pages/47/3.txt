“Hang me if I saw him sitting there, Nattie! I know it’s deuced awkward for you.
But I hope you’ll forgive me this once—this very first break. Why, what’s the
matter?”
“Don’t touch me; don’t come near me,” she returned angrily. “What do you mean by
entering the house without ringing?”
“I came in with your brother, as I often do,” he answered coldly, in self-justification.
“We came in the side way. He went upstairs and I came in here hoping to find you.
