But he had scrawled on a piece of paper that lay in the lamplight:
“I love you. Good-by—because I love you.”
Edna grew faint when she read the words. She went and sat on the sofa. Then&nbsp;she stretched herself out there, never uttering a sound. She did not sleep.&nbsp;She did not go to bed. The lamp sputtered and went out. She was still awake&nbsp;in the morning, when Celestine unlocked the kitchen door and came in to light&nbsp;the fire.
