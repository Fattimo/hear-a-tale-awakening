A hundred times she had read over the letter with which the locket had come&nbsp;back to her. No later than that morning she had again pored over it. As she&nbsp;sat beside the window, smoothing the letter out upon her knee, heavy and spiced&nbsp;odors stole in to her with the songs of birds and the humming of insects in&nbsp;the air.
