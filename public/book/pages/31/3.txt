On the floor was a fresh matting, covered with a rug&nbsp;or two; and on the walls hung a few tasteful pictures. But the room was filled&nbsp;with flowers. These were a surprise to her. Arobin had sent them, and had&nbsp;had Celestine distribute them during Edna’s absence. Her bedroom was adjoining,&nbsp;and across a small passage were the dining-room and kitchen.
