That’s how come&nbsp;Frenchy got permoted an never got a scratch sence he’s been in the ranks.&nbsp;Hey, French! aint I right?” Edmond looked up absently from his letter.
“What is it?” he asked.
“Aint that a charm you got round your neck?”
“It must be, Nick,” returned Edmond with a smile. “I don’t know how I could&nbsp;have gone through this year and a half without it.”
The letter had made Edmond heart sick and home sick. He stretched himself&nbsp;on his back and looked straight up at the blinking stars. But he was not thinking&nbsp;of them nor of anything but a certain spring day when the bees were humming&nbsp;in the clematis; when a girl was saying good bye to him.
