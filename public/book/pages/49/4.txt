“Git your duds! you! Frenchy!” Nick was bellowing in his face. There was what&nbsp;appeared to be a scramble and a rush rather than any regulated movement. The&nbsp;hill side was alive with clatter and motion; with sudden up-springing lights&nbsp;among the pines. In the east the dawn was unfolding out of the darkness. Its&nbsp;glimmer was yet dim in the plain below.
“What’s it all about?” wondered a big black bird perched in the top of the&nbsp;tallest tree. He was an old solitary and a wise one, yet he was not wise enough&nbsp;to guess what it was all about. So all day long he kept blinking and wondering.
