The moments glided on, while a feeling of good fellowship passed around the&nbsp;circle like a mystic cord, holding and binding these people together with&nbsp;jest and laughter. Monsieur Ratignolle was the first to break the pleasant&nbsp;charm. At ten o’clock he excused himself. Madame Ratignolle was waiting for&nbsp;him at home. She was bien souffrante, and she was filled with vague dread,&nbsp;which only her husband’s presence could allay.
