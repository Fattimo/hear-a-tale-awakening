The young mother was recovering slowly, and lay full length, in her soft white&nbsp;muslins and laces, upon a couch. The baby was beside her, upon her arm, where&nbsp;he had fallen asleep, at her breast. The yellow nurse woman sat beside a window&nbsp;fanning herself.
Madame Valmondé bent her portly figure over Désirée and kissed her, holding&nbsp;her an instant tenderly in her arms. Then she turned to the child.
