He seated himself again and rolled a cigarette, which he smoked in silence.&nbsp;Neither did Mrs. Pontellier speak. No multitude of words could have been more&nbsp;significant than those moments of silence, or more pregnant with the first-felt&nbsp;throbbings of desire.
When the voices of the bathers were heard approaching, Robert said good-night.&nbsp;She did not answer him. He thought she was asleep. Again she watched his figure&nbsp;pass in and out of the strips of moonlight as he walked away.
