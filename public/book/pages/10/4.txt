Her unlooked-for achievement was the subject of wonder, applause, and admiration.&nbsp;Each one congratulated himself that his special teachings had accomplished&nbsp;this desired end.
“How easy it is!” she thought. “It is nothing,” she said aloud; “why did I&nbsp;not discover before that it was nothing. Think of the time I have lost splashing&nbsp;about like a baby!” She would not join the groups in their sports and bouts,&nbsp;but intoxicated with her newly conquered power, she swam out alone.
