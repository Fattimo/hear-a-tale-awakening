     She waited for the material pictures which she thought would gather and
blaze before her imagination. She waited in vain. She saw no pictures of solitude,
of hope, of longing, or of despair. But the very passions themselves were
aroused within her soul, swaying it, lashing it, as the waves daily beat upon
her splendid body. She trembled, she was choking, and the tears blinded her.
