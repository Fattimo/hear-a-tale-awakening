They played a duet from “Zampa,” and at the earnest solicitation&nbsp;of every one present followed it with the overture to “The Poet and the Peasant.”
“Allez vous-en! Sapristi!” shrieked the parrot outside the door. He was the&nbsp;only being present who possessed sufficient candor to admit that he was not&nbsp;listening to these gracious performances for the first time that summer.
