“Well, good-by, à jeudi,” said Mr. Pontellier, as he let himself out.
The Doctor would have liked during the course of conversation to ask, “Is&nbsp;there any man in the case?” but he knew his Creole too well to make such a&nbsp;blunder as that.
He did not resume his book immediately, but sat for a while meditatively looking&nbsp;out into the garden.
