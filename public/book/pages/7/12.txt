At another time her affections were deeply engaged by a young gentleman who&nbsp;visited a lady on a neighboring plantation. It was after they went to Mississippi&nbsp;to live. The young man was engaged to be married to the young lady, and they&nbsp;sometimes called upon Margaret, driving over of afternoons in a buggy. Edna&nbsp;was a little miss, just merging into her teens; and the realization that she&nbsp;herself was nothing, nothing, nothing to the engaged young man was a bitter&nbsp;affliction to her.
