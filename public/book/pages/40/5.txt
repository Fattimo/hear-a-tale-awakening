“Non, non!” the woman laughed. “Don’t you look fo’ no deer, Chéri. Dat’s too&nbsp;big. But you bring La Folle one good fat squirrel fo’ her dinner to-morrow,&nbsp;an’ she goin’ be satisfi’.”
“One squirrel ain’t a bite. I’ll bring you mo’ ’an one, La Folle,” he had&nbsp;boasted pompously as he went away.
