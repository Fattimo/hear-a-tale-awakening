He resented&nbsp;the intrusion of the children, who gaped with wondering eyes at him, sitting&nbsp;so stiff up there in their mother’s bright atelier. When they drew near he&nbsp;motioned them away with an expressive action of the foot, loath to disturb&nbsp;the fixed lines of his countenance, his arms, or his rigid shoulders.
Edna, anxious to entertain him, invited Mademoiselle Reisz to meet him, having&nbsp;promised him a treat in her piano playing; but Mademoiselle declined the invitation.&nbsp;So together they attended a soirée musicale at the Ratignolles’. Monsieur&nbsp;and Madame Ratignolle made much of the Colonel, installing him as the guest&nbsp;of honor and engaging him at once to dine with them the following Sunday,&nbsp;or any day which he might select.
