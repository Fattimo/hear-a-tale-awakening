There were one or two men whom she observed at the soirée musicale; but she&nbspwould never have felt moved to any kittenish display to attract their notice—to&nbspany feline or feminine wiles to express herself toward them. Their personality&nbspattracted her in an agreeable way. Her fancy selected them, and she was glad&nbspwhen a lull in the music gave them an opportunity to meet her and talk with&nbspher. Often on the street the glance of strange eyes had lingered in her memory,&nbspand sometimes had disturbed her.
