The Doctor doubled&nbsp;his old-fashioned cloak across his breast as he strode home through the darkness.&nbsp;He knew his fellow-creatures better than most men; knew that inner life which&nbsp;so seldom unfolds itself to unanointed eyes. He was sorry he had accepted&nbsp;Pontellier’s invitation. He was growing old, and beginning to need rest and&nbsp;an imperturbed spirit.
