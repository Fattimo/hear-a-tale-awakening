     Mrs. Sommers was one who knew the value of bargains; who could stand
for hours making her way inch by inch toward the desired object that was selling
below cost. She could elbow her way if need be; she had learned to clutch
a piece of goods and hold it and stick to it with persistence and determination
till her turn came to be served, no matter when it came.
